What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy refers to the ability to access, understand, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies.

It includes skills like using email, digital payments, online applications, cybersecurity awareness, and critical thinking in identifying misinformation.

Digital Divide in India (2025)

How to Create Email ID

Step 1: Visit Gmail.com

How to Use UPI Safely

Use official apps like BHIM, PhonePe, Google Pay.
Never scan unknown QR codes.
Never approve collect request unless verified.
Never share UPI PIN.

Identify Fake Messages

Check spelling mistakes.
Avoid urgent reward messages.
Verify source website domain.
Never click unknown links.
